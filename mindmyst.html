Crossword Puzzle is a word-based puzzle game where the goal is to complete a grid with correct answers. The rules are as follows:

  • Single Session Only: You must complete the puzzle in one session. Refreshing or leaving the page will result in disqualification.
  • Timer: You have 10 minutes (600 seconds) to complete each level. Once the timer ends, the "Check" and "Submit" buttons will be disabled.
  • Check Attempts: You can check your answers up to 10 times during the game. Each check deducts from the remaining attempts, so use them wisely. The "Check" button will highlight correct answers in green and incorrect answers in red to guide your review.
  • Answer Validation: Enter your answers in the input boxes provided. Ensure your answers are correct before submitting. Incorrect entries will be highlighted for review.

  • Progression: Solve the puzzle correctly within the time limit to proceed to the next level. Incomplete or incorrect puzzles will require further review before submission.
  • Respect Time and Attempts: Keep track of the remaining time and your check attempts, as these are limited resources.
  • Enjoy and Play Fair: This is a test of your skills and attention to detail. Compete fairly and have fun!
